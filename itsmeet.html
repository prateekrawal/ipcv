First Industry-Academia Meet-up on
Intelligent Vehicles and Intelligent Transportation Systems
About

We envision this meet-up to be a platform for academicians, researchers and industry professionals to share innovative ideas, state-of-the-art, issues, challenges and successes in the areas of Intelligent Vehicles and Intelligent Transportation Systems.

This event co-located with the 1st International Conference on Signal Processing VLSI and Communication Engineering (ICSPVCE 2019)that is scheduled from 28th to 30th March 2019.
