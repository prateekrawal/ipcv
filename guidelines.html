Instructions for preparing Camera-Ready Paper

5. Revise the manuscript according to reviewers� comments and proof read your source document thoroughly to ensure that all reviewer comments are incorporated. Please prepare a camera ready manuscript with a maximum page limit of 6, A4 size pages without author biography. A maximum of 8 pages is allowed with a charge of Rs 1000/- per extra page. Papers more than 8 pages will not be included for publishing at IEEE Xplore

a) For IEEE conference template, (A4 doc file) follow this link:
http://www.ieee.org/conferences_events/conferences/publishing/templates.html

b) Your camera ready manuscript should be complete in all respects, including names of all authors, contact details and their affiliation as per above IEEE conference format.

c) Add the appropriate �Copyright Notice� to the bottom of the first page of your paper/ source document as foot note. Copyright notice to be added is given below. To see where the copyright notice will appear in the paper, click on the following: Sample Paper

d) The title of the paper must not be changed for the camera ready version, unless it is to correct some typographical errors, or the change is suggested in the review process. Any change must also be reflected in the relevant CMT paper submission text record that was created when the paper ID was generated.

e) Authors using the LaTeX template must use the following package options: 10pt, final,conference, letterpaper. Authors using software other than the TeX/LaTeX system should match the pdf generated in keeping with IEEE guidelines available on their web page.

f) Convert the Camera Ready manuscript file created using Step a and Step b to its .pdf version. Name the file as �ICSVPCEXXX.pdf (paper number.pdf�)
(For example if the paper number in CMT is Paper Id #185, the .pdf file of the Camera Ready manuscript should be ICSVPCE185.pdf)

g) The .pdf file created in step-f should be checked using IEEE pdf express for its IEEE Xplore compatibility at https://www.pdf-express.org/. The conference ID for PDF eXpress is 37817XP

h) After successful generation of IEEE Xplore compatible Camera Ready manuscript PDF file, upload the PDF file through your login in CMT account for IICIP 2019 (see step 7). Please note that the paper MUST have similarity index less than 10% as reported by any standard plagiarism check (we recommend the use of turnitin) tool. In case the paper's similarity index is greater than 10%, please CAREFULLY rewrite your manuscript. This is a mandatory requirement for inclusion of the paper in the conference proceedings.

i) Without the copyright form, the paper will NOT be published in IEEE Xplore.
