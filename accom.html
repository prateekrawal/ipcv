Accomodation Details

During your stay in New Delhi for the conference, it is advised to book a hotel or other accommodations which are near to the university, to avoid wastage of time. While you can stay at any hotel of your preference, we personally recommend the following for their reasonable prices and the least travel time to DTU. Also, all the mentioned accommodations have AC, free Wifi and complimentary breakfast.

1. Hotel Premier Inn

Website: www.global.premierinn.com

Address: Shalimar Bagh District Centre, Haiderpur Crossing, Outer Ring Road, New Delhi-110088

2. Hotel Sandberry (OYO Premium Rohini)

Website: www.hotelsandberry.com

Address: D-14, Prashant Vihar, Main Outer Ring Road, Rohini Sector-14, New Delhi-110085

3. Sunrize Guest House

Website: www.sunrizeguesthouse.com

Address: 8-B/10, Rohini Sector-15, New Delhi-110089

*Due to limited availability of rooms, it is advised to confirm your bookings at the earliest.

Those who wish to book an accommodation of their own can use the following information:-

  1. Delhi Technological University is well connected from Rithala metro station on Red Line and Samaypur Badli metro station on Yellow Line.

  2. The nearby areas to DTU are Rohini, Pitampura and Shalimar Bagh.
